    ABOUT THE COURSE

    The Fundamental Analysis Bootcamp is a beginner-friendly, hands-on journey into the world of stock market investing. Over 2 weeks, participants will learn to decode financial statements, understand valuation metrics, and assess a company’s true worth — making them confident and self-sufficient investors by the end of the course.
